Activities
The beaches

In the shade of the canisses, the Moune beach is located 100m from the hotel. It is the only real beach located in the town of Gassin. Partly private and dedicated to the sailing school, the atmosphere is relaxed and sporty.

The Treizain beach, 3 minutes by car from the hotel, offers a friendly place to relax. This natural beach offers a view of Saint-Tropez to the east, Sainte-Maxime to the north and the entire gulf.

La Bouillabaisse welcomes you on the edge of the town of Saint-Tropez with a beautiful area of fine sand and offers a splendid view of the coast and the Maures massif with magical sunsets.

More activities

Hiking lovers, the region is full of places to discover.

Numerous mountain bike circuits and hiking trails can be found in the vicinity and offer splendid views of the Gulf of Saint-Tropez, the Bay of Cavalaire, the Maures region, the villages of Gassin, Grimaud and many others

For water sports lovers … In the bends of the Treizain, the sailing school of La Moune offers courses or rentals of windsurfing, kayaks, fun board …

And if you are looking for a good table, a private beach or an idea for an outing, our team is there to recommend the best spots in Gassin and the surrounding area.
